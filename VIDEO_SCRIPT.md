# Stead — 90-second demo script

> **Roles (say these once):** the **app is the control panel** (Mom). **MARS is the interaction point** (Ruby — the robot she relates to). The bridge between them is **consent**.

---

### 0:00–0:10 — The problem (hook)
*On screen: Ruby speaking; a general speech-to-text mangles it ("the same **Oppo**…", repetition, garble).*

**VO:** "This is Ruby. She has cerebral palsy — and the world's AI literally **can't hear her.** Millions like her are invisible to the AI revolution."

### 0:10–0:25 — The fix: a *sovereign* model
*On screen: the loss curve **2.12 → 0.53**; caption "fine-tuned on a Nebius H100, on HER voice, owned by her family."*

**VO:** "So we trained her a **sovereign** model — on her own recordings, on Nebius GPU, owned by her family, never handed to big tech. Now her agent understands **her.**"

### 0:25–0:50 — Agency: she runs her own day
*On screen: the **control panel** + **MARS**. Ruby asks through MARS; the agent acts; the day fills in.*

**VO:** "Ruby runs her own day. She asks — and her agent **schedules her care, orders dinner, even researches a specialist and calls to verify insurance** — all on her behalf."

### 0:50–1:10 — The money shot: safe by consent
*On screen: an out-of-bounds request → **HALT** → Mom's control panel → **grant / revoke live** → the audit ledger streaming.*

**VO:** "And the instant she reaches past what her family allowed, it **stops and asks Mom** — who grants or revokes, live. Every action audited. Power she can use, **because the people who love her hold the keys.**"

### 1:10–1:25 — The stack (sponsors, on-thesis)
*On screen: Nebius · Composio · DGX Spark · Plumbline.*

**VO:** "Train sovereign on **Nebius**. Act safely through **Composio**. Serve sovereign on a **DGX Spark**. And we open-sourced **Plumbline** — the tool that hardens any agent acting on someone's behalf. It found real bugs in ours, and we fixed them."

### 1:25–1:30 — Altitude
*On screen: "AI for everyone — finally including the people it forgot."*

**VO:** "This is AI for everyone. **Finally including the people it forgot.**"

---

**Show these links:** the live app (Cloud Run URL) · `github.com/alisoncossette/plumbline` · the loss curve + the 7 MB sovereign adapter.

**Tone:** warm, certain, specific. The emotional beat is the HALT — a machine acting for a vulnerable person, and a guardian able to stop it instantly.
